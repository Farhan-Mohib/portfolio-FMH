SAM-Augmented Blending for Enhanced Microplastic Detection Using YOLO11

Published Research Paper  |  IEEE STI 2025 Best Paper Award

Developed novel synthetic data augmentation framework addressing underwater microplastic detection using advanced computer vision and deep learning. Leveraged Segment Anything Model (SAM) for automated mask generation, enabling intelligent synthetic dataset creation through blending and copy-paste augmentation techniques. Trained YOLOv11 models on augmented datasets and demonstrated 2.89% improvement in detection accuracy (mAP@0.5:0.95).

Key Achievements: Peer-reviewed publication · Novel augmentation methodology · Real-world environmental application · Demonstrated accuracy improvements in oceanographic monitoring systems

Published Paper · IEEE STI 2025 DOI: 10.1109/STI69347.2025.11367502

Mavirov ROV – Leviathan Underwater Drone

Co-founder & Software Team Lead | Production Deployment | Real-World Field Operations

Architected and implemented comprehensive software ecosystem for the Leviathan ROV — Mavirov team's first underwater drone. Designed hierarchical control architecture integrating Pixhawk autopilot with Raspberry Pi 5 central processing hub managing real-time video streaming, telemetry aggregation, and servo coordination. System supports PS5 joystick control, keyboard fine-grain servo adjustment, and operator-directed underwater navigation.

🌊 Real-World Field Operations & Deployment

Milestone Achievement: Successfully conducted live underwater field operations at Postogola Cantonment, monitoring and capturing diver movements at approximately 33 feet depth.
