SAM-Augmented Blending for Enhanced Microplastic Detection Using YOLO11

Published Research Paper | IEEE STI 2025 Best Paper Award

Developed novel synthetic data augmentation framework addressing underwater microplastic detection using advanced computer vision and deep learning. Leveraged Segment Anything Model (SAM) for automated mask generation, enabling intelligent synthetic dataset creation through blending and copy-paste augmentation techniques. Trained YOLOv11 models on augmented datasets and demonstrated 2.89% improvement in detection accuracy (mAP@0.5:0.95).
